EMOTION DETECTION

Emotion detection using deep learning involves training neural networks to recognize and interpret human emotions from various sources such as text, images, or audio. These models learn patterns and features that correspond to different emotional states, enabling them to classify input data into categories like happiness, sadness, anger, and more. Through extensive data training and optimization, they become adept at capturing subtle cues and nuances, offering valuable insights for applications in fields like healthcare, marketing, and human-computer interaction.
